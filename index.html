Key Features

  • Key Regulatory registers

    All your key regulatory registers held in one place, including Complaints, Promotions and Breaches.

  • Automated KPIs and One-to-Ones

    KPIs are automatically calculated and one-to-one visit documents are generated using data from throughout the system.

  • File Selection

    Cases can be automatically selected for checking, based on adviser licences and other predefined rules, or called manually.

  • File Checking Process

    A single portal for files to be uploaded, reviewed and graded, giving a consistent file checking process.

  • Document Library

    A central repository for your documentation, such as templates, guides and policies, including version control.

  • Regulatory Reporting

    RegData reports are automatically generated from Turnover and New Business registers.

  • Key Management Information

    A suite of reports that draws information from across the system, providing oversight of firms and advisers.

  • Audit Trail of Oversight and Monitoring

    Automatically named and dated notes can be recorded providing evidence of communication and decision-making.

Our Background

TriTech Compli Limited was formed to provide hands-on, pragmatic support to financial advice firms, or those overseeing such firms, in meeting their regulatory responsibilities through effective oversight of firms and advisers.

Our software has been monitoring firms and advisers for more than 10 years in a regulatory environment and continues to evolve in an ever-increasing regulatory market. It is built to be intuitive for both the advisers and those providing oversight functions; efficiencies driven by automation have been key to our design decisions.

Our team has been involved in developing and running the software since its inception.
